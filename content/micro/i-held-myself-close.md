---
title: "I held myself close to the large cross where the priest gave mass"
date: 2020-09-01
tags: ["english"]
author: "Vicente Manuel Muñoz Milchorena"
---

I held myself close to the large cross where the priest gave mass, I watched with contempt as the priest approached me, those piercing red eyes shined from the shadows as he crossed through the pillars of the nave, with a grin he revealed his power to me and I froze.
