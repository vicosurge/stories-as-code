---
title: "When I was in first grade I pretended to have camera eyes"
date: 2020-01-01
tags: ["english"]
author: "Vicente Manuel Muñoz Milchorena"
---

When I was in first grade I pretended to have camera eyes, I would take pictures of the important stuff going around in the class, when I was finished I would take out the camera roll from my right temple and deposit it on an invisible box next to my desk.
