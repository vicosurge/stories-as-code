I travel through the eyes of Medusa, my body remains trapped in stone, safely, while I visit strange worlds and creatures, all thanks to my disbelief on an old legend, she is here to protect us, not hurt us, she has another side known to a select few. /1

Travelers are protected by mother Coatlicue, Mistress of Snakes, traveling through both arms, I am safe, from the womb of the world back to this one, a reflection of the Hermetic truth, as above, as below, seen through the Obsidian Mirror of Tezcatlipoca. /2

I see the jaguar, shining jade and emerald of the jungle, a mouth that reveals to me the secret word of grandfather Quetzalcoatl, I see the rainbow colors of its tail, and the spider claw holding the heart of the jungle atop the sacred mountain and temple. /3

I am the Mage, the epicenter of change, through me flows the heart of the jungle, of mother Coatlicue, father Tezcatlipoca and grandfather Quetzalcoatl, golden streams leading to Tlaloc and the tears of the sky that feed their sons, they cry, unable to help. /4

Cry, do it joyfully, our ancient deities walk here with us, I declare so myself, the three, nine and once wise Mage, that their names never be forgotten, forged into our minds forever, so be it, this is my will and I make it be so. /5
