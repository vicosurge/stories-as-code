title: "After traveling for days the two bronze pillars in the desert"
date: 2020-01-01
tags: ["english"]
author: "Vicente Manuel Muñoz Milchorena"
---

After traveling for days the two bronze pillars in the desert were at sight, between them sat a figure, as the traveler approached it the figure became as tall as the pillars, raising its right hand it said: "Who are you, where do you come from and where are you going?"
