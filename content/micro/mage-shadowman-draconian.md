---

The Mage is the light side, the representation of Chaos, there is calculated thought in what seems to be madness, fire is the core element as it represents the eternal renewal that goes through the right hand.

The Shadowman is the dark side, the representation of a hidden Order, through the macabre words there is a need for absolute control on everything around it, represented by the element of wind which acts without being seen through the left hand.

The Draconian is the middle ground, the representative of the Ain Soph Aur manifested through water, the will of the Draconian is the fluidity of knowledge through the element of water, its two subordinates work in conjunction to make the will come through.

I am the element of Earth, I am that which has spoken the first word and has become manifested physically, I am the Foundation of the tree, from it spawn the remaining Sefirot, I am the flaming star, the tetragrammaton, the will manifested.

Below and to the sides are the Knight and the Stellarman, the Knight is the willingness to defend, to protect, to be violent, stubbornness, archaic; the Stellarman is the representation of curiosity, of knowledge, creativity, growth, exploration.
