---
title: "The three pedestals held a skull each"
date: 2019-05-01
tags: ["english", "esoteric"]
author: "Vicente Manuel Muñoz Milchorena"
---

The three pedestals held a skull each, in front of the pedestals a chair in which a different figure took seat, surrounding them a large Solomonic seal made from crushed lapis lazuli, deep orange eyes shining in the dark waiting for the young woman to ask.
