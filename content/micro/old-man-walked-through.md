---
title: "The old man walked through the forest on days with thick fog"
date: 2018-11-01
tags: ["english"]
author: "Vicente Manuel Muñoz Milchorena"
---

The old man walked through the forest on days with thick fog, he carried a wooden ring with twelve small bells attached to it, he would ring them constantly to attract travellers lost on the woods and take them home to his home for a hot drink while they waited
