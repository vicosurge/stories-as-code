---
title: "The hard wind dusts me"
date: 2020-01-01
tags: ["english", "poetry"]
author: "Vicente Manuel Muñoz Milchorena"
---

The hard wind dusts me,
my soul, mind, heart,
gives me courage to explore
what I forced to a corner,
that calls with every gust
ignored, fearful memories
help me know myself,
hurt myself, beat myself
into understanding that
I wronged, I hurt, I left
I am me again
