---
title: "I sit now on the throne at the top of the emerald tower"
date: 2020-09-01
tags: ["english", "esoteric", "monarchy"]
author: "Vicente Manuel Muñoz Milchorena"
---

I sit now on the throne at the top of the emerald tower, the bodies of the enemies of my father litter the ground around it, the old king beheaded, his blood cleansed the fields below, I am here to gaze at the world and the moon as my new domains.
