---
title: "As we harvest for the season we remember"
date: 2020-01-01
tags: ["english", "melancholy", "poetry"]
author: "Vicente Manuel Muñoz Milchorena"
---

As we harvest for the season we remember,
those who left us untimely, a heart barren
of emotions, to grief, to laugh, to joy,
we are hardened, re-learning to one day
let our children enjoy a brighter future
