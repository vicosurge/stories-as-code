There used to be a small detention center over at 8th Street in Downtown Tijuana, people will not know the intricacies of the infamous drunk tank of Downtown and how people would go there on purpose. /1

Two friends always had an emergency plan, one knew that his wife would beat the shit out of him, he'd rather end up in the drunk tank than go home at six in the morning, the other preferred this than have his car towed away due to his inebriated state. /2

In the end this master plan would work out perfectly, they would start a fight with each other, get really rowdy and police would intervene, police being more interested in quotas than the actual reason of their fight, in the long run everyone won something /3

This all ended many years ago when town hall decided this place had to go, it spoke about a decadent era and this would no longer be the case, people protested this, these two guys were there, it went down anyway and a park was built where it used to be. /4
