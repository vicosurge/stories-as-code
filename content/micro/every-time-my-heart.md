---
title: "Every time my heart aches"
date: 2020-03-01
tags: ["english", "melancholy", "poetry"]
author: "Vicente Manuel Muñoz Milchorena"
---

Every time my heart aches
my soul feels lighter
with these tears like stone rolling down
a dead river I become
little to care, little to see
but my facade of a thousand masks I keep
they mustn't know, they wouldn’t care
that what they say hurts me again
