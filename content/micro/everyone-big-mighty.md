---
title: "Everyone is big and mighty"
date: 2020-01-01
tags: ["english", "monarchy", "poetry"]
author: "Vicente Manuel Muñoz Milchorena"
---

Everyone is big and mighty,
everyone says they'll take it on,
then they have to live an hour
inside the throne room, all because
that very, very spicy salsa that no
matter how hard you tried, you just
had to keep getting a piece of it,
now it has a piece of you
