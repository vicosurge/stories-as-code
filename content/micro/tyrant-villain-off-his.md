---
title: "Tyrant, villain, off with his head"
date: 2019-05-01
tags: ["english", "monarchy"]
author: "Vicente Manuel Muñoz Milchorena"
---

"Tyrant, villain, off with his head" The people cried, the King saw from the balcony the mass at the walls. "Have it then" He removed his crown and threw it at the mass, they tore each other apart for it, "A wise move my liege, let them fight while you rule!"
