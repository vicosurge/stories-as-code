---
title: "Too anxious to sit down, too much coffee"
date: 2020-01-01
tags: ["english", "introspective", "poetry"]
author: "Vicente Manuel Muñoz Milchorena"
---

Too anxious to sit down, too much coffee
sugar, thoughts, water, food, oxygen, life
too much of everything is bad, but I need
to keep running, pushing, yelling, doing
anything I can to not waste anymore time
that I could be using to build a new tomorrow
