---
title: "The focus of this city was to impress"
date: 2021-05-01
tags: ["cyberpunk", "english"]
author: "Vicente Manuel Muñoz Milchorena"
---

The focus of this city was to impress, to enthrall, hypnotize with neon, dark mirrors and pure concrete, to hide deep within its bowels horrors that could not be spoken, of complete abuse, silence and slavery.
