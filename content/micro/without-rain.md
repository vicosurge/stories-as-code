---
title: "without the rain"
date: 2020-03-01
tags: ["english", "poetry"]
author: "Vicente Manuel Muñoz Milchorena"
---

without the rain
these barren lands
show no remorse
and no respite
on human hearts
on human minds
on human life
because at high noon
with crooked eyes
only red and black
but more important
a broken man
