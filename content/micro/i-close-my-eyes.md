---
title: "As I close my eyes to sleep I see the horrors"
date: 2020-09-01
tags: ["english", "esoteric"]
author: "Vicente Manuel Muñoz Milchorena"
---

As I close my eyes to sleep I see the horrors, the twisted madness that sleeps in our subconscious, monstrosity that pulsates with anger, looking to destroy all of our sanity, will, self, I fight it through the emerald gates hoping to one day live in peace.
